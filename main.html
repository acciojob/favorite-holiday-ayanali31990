Paris, France


Paris, often known as the "City of Light," is famous for its rich history, art, and culture. Visitors from around the world flock to this romantic destination to experience iconic landmarks, world-class museums, and exceptional cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic landmark that defines the Paris skyline.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the famous Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre - A charming district known for its artistic heritage and beautiful views of the city.
  5. Sainte-Chapelle - A breathtaking medieval chapel with stunning stained glass windows.
  • Croissant - A deliciously flaky and buttery French pastry.
  • Baguette - The classic French bread often enjoyed with cheese and wine.
  • Escargots de Bourgogne - A daring but delicious French dish of snails cooked in garlic butter.
  • Macarons - Colorful and delicate meringue-based cookies filled with buttercream.
